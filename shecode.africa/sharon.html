NIKOLA TESLA

Caption: NIKOLA TESLA

The genius inventor and electrical engineer.

Timeline

  • 1856 - Born on July 10th in Smiljan, Austrian Empire (now Croatia).
  • 1875 - Enrolled at the Austrian Polytechnic in Graz to study electrical engineering.
  • 1884 - Immigrated to the United States and began working with Thomas Edison.
  • 1887 - Founded the Tesla Electric Company.
  • 1891 - Invented the Tesla coil.
  • 1893 - Demonstrated wireless energy transmission at the World's Columbian Exposition in Chicago.
  • 1895 - Conducted experiments with X-rays.
  • 1898 - Demonstrated the first remote-controlled boat.
  • 1901 - Patented the Tesla turbine.
  • 1931 - Passed away on January 7th in New York City.
